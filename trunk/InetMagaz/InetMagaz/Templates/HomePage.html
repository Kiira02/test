Konami Launches Metal Gear Art Studio and Contest
Posted on 18 February 2013
Konami has just launched Metal Gear Art Studio, a website where fans can post their Metal Gear fan art as well as participate in contests drawing series characters. The first contest focuses on Raiden and runs from February 18 to the 24, and will be followed in turn by "Mechs and Vehicles," "Snake," "Female Characters," and "Male Characters" contests.
